Privacy Policy for PDF Nastaliq

Effective Date: May 21, 2024

At PDF Nastaliq, accessible from https://pdf-nastaliq.vercel.app, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded and how we use it.

1. Data Collection

We do NOT store your data on any external server or database.

PDF Nastaliq is a client-side tool. This means:

  • Text & Assignments: Everything you type is saved locally in your browser (using LocalStorage). It is only visible to you.
  • Images: Any images you upload are converted to a temporary format (Base64) and saved in your browser session.

2. Third Party Services

Currently, we do not use third-party services to process your data. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

3. Cookies

We use local storage to save your settings and draft assignments so you don't lose them if you refresh the page. This data does not leave your device.

4. User's Rights

Since your data is stored on your device, you have full control over it.

  • To delete your data: Simply click the "Clear" button in the tool, or clear your browser cache/history.
  • To export: Use the PDF download button to save your work to your device.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
