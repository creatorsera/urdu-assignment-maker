Privacy Policy

PDF Nastaliq is built with a simple principle: your writing belongs to you. We don't collect it, store it, or see it — ever.

Effective Date: May 21, 2024
No servers
No accounts
No tracking
No cookies
100% free
Section 01
What Data We Collect

Short answer: nothing. PDF Nastaliq is a fully client-side application. No data is ever sent to, or stored on, any external server.

Everything you type — your assignment text, names, subjects, dates — is processed entirely inside your browser and saved locally on your own device using localStorage. It is invisible to us.

Text & assignments — saved in your browser's localStorage only. Never transmitted.
Images you upload — converted to Base64 and stored locally in your browser. They never leave your device.
Settings & preferences (font size, layout, etc.) — stored locally for convenience. Not shared.
Section 02
Third-Party Services

PDF Nastaliq does not use any analytics platforms, advertising networks, or third-party tracking scripts.

We load the Noto Nastaliq Urdu and Outfit fonts from Google Fonts. This is a standard web request — Google may log your IP address according to their own privacy policy, but no content from our app is shared in this process.

The app is hosted on Vercel, which may collect basic server logs (IP address, browser type) for infrastructure purposes. These logs are not accessible to us and are governed by Vercel's Privacy Policy.

Section 03
Local Storage (Not Cookies)

We use localStorage — not cookies — to save your drafts and settings so your work is never lost when you close or refresh the browser.

Unlike cookies, localStorage data is never sent to any server. It lives exclusively on your device and is only readable by this website.

Section 04
Your Rights & Control

Since all data is stored on your device, you have complete control over it at all times.

Delete your data

Use the delete button on any document card, or clear your browser's localStorage from browser settings.

Export your work

Use the PDF button to download your assignment to your device at any time.

Access your data

Open browser DevTools → Application → localStorage to view exactly what is stored.

No account needed

There is no registration, login, or profile. Nothing to delete from a server because nothing exists there.

Section 05
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Since we don't collect your contact details, we cannot notify you directly — we recommend bookmarking this page if you'd like to stay informed.

The core principle will never change: your data stays on your device.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how PDF Nastaliq handles data, feel free to reach out through our GitHub repository or by opening an issue.
